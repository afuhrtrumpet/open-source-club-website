    Spring 2008 - First Meeting

    posted: 03-27-2008

    We had our first meeting for the quarter in Dreese Labs room 266 at 7:00 pm. Meetings end whenever they end, with no specific time. No absolute topic was decided on, so we discussed a few random things, such as South Park creators Trey Parker and Matt Stone streaming South Park online for free (where we legally watched a recent episode from Season 12, which does play in Linux without DRM hacks), Adobe creating a web-based free express (and watered down) version of Photoshop, we also spent a few minutes sharing our web-development views. The meeting was open to students and non-students alike, and pizza was served as always. Altogether we had a great turnout and I look forward to a similar turnout at our next meeting.
